About Us

We are a small software startup passionate about open-source and empowering software engineers.

At Bansari Tech, we are dedicated to creating open-source hobby projects that inspire innovation and collaboration. We believe in the power of the community and strive to contribute to the open-source ecosystem.

We go beyond just software development. Our team of experienced professionals is passionate about helping individuals solve complex software problems and guiding them to make informed decisions. We provide consulting services to businesses, offering expert advice and technical expertise to drive successful software projects.

In addition, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of software engineers. We offer mentorship and training programs to aspiring developers, exposing them to real-world industrial projects and teaching them the best practices and methodologies used in the industry.

With our expertise and passion for open-source software, we aim to make a positive impact by empowering individuals and promoting collaboration within the software development community.
